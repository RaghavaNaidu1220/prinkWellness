How to Use

❄️Store Properly

Store your PrinkWellness suppositories in a cool, dry place. Refrigerate before use to ensure the suppository is firm for easier insertion.

🧼

Wash Your Hands

Before handling the suppository, wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water.

📦

Remove Packaging

Remove the suppository from its packaging. If it feels too soft, place it back in the refrigerator for a few minutes.

🛌

Find a Comfortable Position

Lie on your back with knees bent or stand with one leg raised on a chair or toilet seat, whichever is more comfortable.

👆

Insert the Suppository

Gently insert the suppository into your vagina as far as is comfortable, similar to inserting a tampon.

⏱️

Remain Lying Down

If possible, remain lying down for 15–20 minutes to allow the suppository to melt and the active ingredients to be absorbed.
