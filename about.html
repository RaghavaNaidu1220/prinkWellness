About PrinkWellness

Pioneering a new approach to menstrual wellness through innovation, science, and nature.

Our Story

PrinkWellness was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize how women experience menstrual pain. Our founder, after years of struggling with debilitating menstrual cramps and finding no satisfactory solutions in the market, decided to create a product that would provide targeted relief without the side effects of conventional medications.

Working with a team of scientists, Ayurvedic practitioners, and women's health experts, we developed India's first clinically validated vaginal suppository specifically designed to address menstrual cramps at their source.

Today, PrinkWellness stands at the forefront of feminine wellness innovation, committed to empowering women through science-backed, nature-derived solutions that prioritize both efficacy and safety.
