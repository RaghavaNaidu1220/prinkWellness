Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products scientifically tested?

+

Yes, all PrinkWellness products undergo rigorous scientific testing and clinical validation before being brought to market. We conduct both laboratory testing and human trials to ensure safety and efficacy.

How quickly can I expect relief from menstrual pain?

+

In our clinical studies, 87% of women reported significant pain relief within 30 minutes of using our vaginal suppository. Individual results may vary based on pain severity and personal physiology.

Are there any side effects?

+

Our clinical trials showed no significant side effects. Unlike oral pain medications, our local delivery system minimizes systemic effects. Some users may experience mild, temporary sensations like warmth or tingling at the application site.

How do plant-based ingredients compare to conventional medications?

+

Our research shows that our plant-based formulation, when delivered locally, provides comparable or superior pain relief to conventional NSAIDs for many women, without the digestive side effects or other systemic concerns associated with oral medications.
