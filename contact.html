Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions.

How do I use PrinkWellness suppositories?

Our suppositories are designed for easy insertion. Each product comes with detailed instructions. For best results, refrigerate before use to ensure the suppository is firm. Wash your hands, lie down, and gently insert the suppository as far as is comfortable. The suppository will melt at body temperature, releasing the active ingredients.

Are PrinkWellness products safe?

Yes, our products are made with 100% natural ingredients and have been clinically validated for safety and efficacy. All our products undergo rigorous testing and are manufactured in facilities that follow strict quality control standards.

How quickly will I feel relief?

Most women report feeling the cooling sensation within 15-20 minutes of insertion, with pain relief following shortly after. The effects typically last for 4-6 hours, though individual experiences may vary.

Can I use PrinkWellness products with tampons or menstrual cups?

We recommend inserting the suppository first and waiting 15-20 minutes before inserting a tampon or menstrual cup. This allows the active ingredients to be absorbed properly before introducing any other products.
