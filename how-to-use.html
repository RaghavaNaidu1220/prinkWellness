How To Use PrinkWellness

Simple instructions for getting the most out of your PrinkWellness products

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to use PrinkWellness vaginal suppositories for maximum relief

1

Store Properly

Store your PrinkWellness suppositories in a cool, dry place. For best results, refrigerate before use to ensure the suppository is firm for easier insertion.

2

Wash Your Hands

Before handling the suppository, wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water.

3

Remove Packaging

Remove the suppository from its packaging. If it feels too soft, place it back in the refrigerator for a few minutes.

4

Find a Comfortable Position

Lie down on your back with your knees bent or stand with one leg raised on a chair or toilet seat, whichever is more comfortable for you.

5

Insert the Suppository

Gently insert the suppository into your vagina as far as is comfortable, similar to inserting a tampon.

6

Remain Lying Down

If possible, remain lying down for 15-20 minutes to allow the suppository to melt and the active ingredients to be absorbed.

Tips for Best Results

Maximize the effectiveness of your PrinkWellness experience

Timing

For best results, use at the first sign of menstrual cramps. The suppository can be used up to 3 times per day during your period.

Menstrual Products

If using with tampons or menstrual cups, insert the suppository first and wait 15-20 minutes before inserting your menstrual product.

Wear a Liner

It's normal for some discharge as the suppository melts. Wearing a panty liner can help manage this.

Hydration

Stay well-hydrated during your period to help maximize the effectiveness of the suppository and support overall comfort.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using PrinkWellness products

How quickly will I feel relief?

Most women report feeling the cooling sensation within 15-20 minutes of insertion, with pain relief following shortly after. The effects typically last for 4-6 hours, though individual experiences may vary.

Is it safe to use during my entire period?

Yes, PrinkWellness suppositories are safe to use throughout your entire period. They can be used up to 3 times per day as needed for pain relief.

Can I use it with other menstrual products?

Yes, PrinkWellness suppositories can be used with tampons, menstrual cups, and pads. For best results, insert the suppository first and wait 15-20 minutes before inserting a tampon or menstrual cup.
